Software Questions

How do the automation tools work?

▼
Our software uses machine learning algorithms to analyze market data, identify opportunities, and execute trades based on predefined parameters. You set your risk tolerance and strategy preferences, and the system handles execution automatically.

Can I use multiple software tools simultaneously?

▼
Yes, depending on your subscription plan. Pro and Enterprise plans allow running multiple strategies simultaneously. Each tool can be configured with different parameters and risk settings.

What exchanges and platforms are supported?

▼
We support major cryptocurrency exchanges and DeFi protocols. Specific integrations vary by software tool. Contact support for a detailed list of supported platforms for your chosen software.

Is there a trial period or demo account?

▼
We offer demo accounts on select plans to test the platform interface. However, live trading requires an active subscription. Contact our sales team for trial options on Enterprise plans.

Payments & Subscriptions

What payment methods do you accept?

▼
We accept major credit cards, cryptocurrency payments (BTC, ETH, USDT), and bank transfers for Enterprise plans. All payments are processed securely through encrypted channels.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

▼
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period. No refunds are provided for partial months.

Are there any hidden fees?

▼
No. The subscription price is all-inclusive. However, you are responsible for exchange fees, network gas fees, and any other costs associated with your trading activities.

Do you offer refunds?

▼
Refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis within the first 7 days of initial subscription, subject to our refund policy terms. Trading losses are not eligible for refunds.

Affiliate Program

How does the affiliate program work?

▼
Earn 25% commission on first-time purchases and 15% on recurring subscriptions. You'll receive a unique referral link to share, and commissions are paid monthly for all referred customers.

▼

Security & Risk

Is my data secure?

▼
Yes. We use bank-level encryption (AES-256) for data storage and SSL/TLS for all transmissions. API keys are encrypted and never stored in plain text. We undergo regular security audits.

Do you guarantee profits?

▼
No. We provide software-based automation tools, not investment advice or guaranteed returns. All trading involves risk, and past performance does not guarantee future results. Users are responsible for their own trading decisions and capital.

What are the risks of using automated trading?

▼
Automated trading carries the same market risks as manual trading, including potential loss of capital. Additional risks include technical failures, connectivity issues, and algorithm limitations. Always use proper risk management and only trade with capital you can afford to lose.
